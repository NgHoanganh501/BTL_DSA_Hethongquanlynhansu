HRM PROJECTS

DATA STRUCTURE:

A. EmployeeDataNode (Linked List) contains:

I, PersonalData
- Name (99 chars)
- Birth (20 chars, dd/mm/yy format)
- Gender (Male/Female)
- NationalID (19 chars)
- TaxNumber (19 chars)
- PhoneNumber (14 chars)
- Email (50 chars)

II, EmploymentContract
- Type (Probation/DefiniteTerm/IndefiniteTerm)
- LaborDate (30 chars, dd/mm/yy - dd/mm/yy or "ongoing" format)
- Position (20 chars)
- Department (20 chars)
- DealtSalary

III, Insurance
- Code (20 chars)
- PayLevel (double)
- Date (30 chars, dd/mm/yy - dd/mm/yy or "ongoing" format)

IV, SalaryManaging
- Base Salary (long long) (equals Dealt Salary - Employment Contract)
- Allowance (long long array) (lunch, transportation, residence)
- Bonus (long long array) (KPI, holidays, projects)
- Tax (double)
- FinalSalary (long long) (Calculated)
- Method (Cash/bankTransfer)

B, TrackingNode (Linked List) contains:
- Day (dd/mm/yy format)
- WorkTimeNode (Linked List) contains:
  + Name (99 chars)
  + Check-in (10 chars, xx:yy format)
  + Check-out (10 chars, xx:yy format)
  + Overtime (int)

C, DayOffNode (Linked List) contains:
- Name (99 chars)
- AbsenceLetter (Yes/No)
- AbsenceDate (30 chars, dd/mm/yy - dd/mm/yy format)
- LetterStatus (Pending/Approved/Rejected)

D, EducationNode (Linked List) contains:
- Name (99 chars)
- CourseName (100 chars)
- CourseTime (int)
- CourseFee (long long)
- CourseStatus (Failed/Ongoing/Passed)

E. LayOffNode (Linked list) contains:
- Name (99 chars)
- Date (20 chars, dd/mm/yy format)
- Lay off reason (Volunteered/Fired/ContractEnded)

F. Human Resource Report (Struct) contains:
- Lay off ratio
- Salary Fee
- Allowance Fee
- Insurance Fee
- Education Fee
